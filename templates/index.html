About the model

This objective of this model is to predict whether future monthly returns on the SP500 will be positive or negative.Data is pulled in through Yahoo Finance (yfinance) and cleaned/evaluated with Pandas. For future months, the models outputs either a 0 (negative monthly return) or a 1 (positive monthly return)
